BEE

Bees was created as an exploration of mobile games to convey social issues. I chose to make a game about bee colony collapse disorder and the global dying of bees. Playing as a bee you race against time to collect enough honey to make it through the winter, but all kinds of threats, such as pesticides and mites, lurk around the corner.

The game is a passion project of mine that I've continually worked on during my studies.I have created everything from the concept to gameplay and art myself. At the moment I'm working on refining the UI, polishing assets and animations and extending my coding skills to program a playable prototype.
